What MedSyn Does

Structured evidence extraction and specialty monitoring — not generic paper summarization.

Specialty NEW & TRENDING Feeds

Browse recent and rising papers organized by medical specialty, so you can stay current without scanning every journal yourself.

Structured Evidence Analysis

AI extracts study design, subjects of study, interventions, outcomes, limitations, and clinical implications into a Study Snapshot — grounded in the abstract or open-access full text, not a free-form summary.

Quality Assessment

Study-type–aware quality checks help you judge methodological strength alongside the extracted evidence.

Clinical Studies

A dedicated rail of recently registered trials from ClinicalTrials.gov, kept alongside literature discovery.

Weekly Specialty Briefings

AI-generated audio briefings by specialty for passive learning during commute or downtime — not per-paper podcasts.

Libraries, CME & NPI

Save papers to a personal library, optionally track CME, and verify NPI as part of clinical identity workflows.

How MedSyn Works

From OpenAlex discovery to structured analysis, specialty feeds, and weekly briefings.

1

Discover

New medical research is identified via OpenAlex and organized into specialty NEW and TRENDING feeds.

2

Analyze

Abstracts and allowed open-access full text are converted into structured evidence fields, MeSH topics, and a quality assessment — not abstract paraphrases.

3

Review

Open a paper’s Study Snapshot and Quality Assessment, link out to the publisher, and browse related clinical studies.

4

Stay Current

Return to specialty feeds and weekly briefing audio to stay synchronized with evolving evidence.
